HOW TO PLAY

Clear the grid of all letters by submitting guesses related to the theme

Successful attempts use every letter in the grid and don't repeat any words

Difficulty scales according to grid size and popularity of the theme

To make a guess, click letters in the order they appear in the word you are guessing. You can only use a cell of the grid once

Press 'Enter Guess' to submit your answer. In the 'Your Guesses' menu, you can click a previous guess to remove it

If you discover every word, you win!
